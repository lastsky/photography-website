Bio

Who Am I?

Raised in Scotland, I grew up with an appreciation for the outdoors. After finishing high school I got my first proper camera, a Pentax ME Super, and my dad taught me the basics of analogue photography. I have been shooting ever since and now use a mix of analogue and digital gear.

I have recently been delving into night-time street photography.
